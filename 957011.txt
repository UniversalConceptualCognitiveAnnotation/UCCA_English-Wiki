Winston Churchill arranged a screening for a party that included Franklin D. Roosevelt and , on its conclusion , addressed the group , saying , " Gentlemen , I thought this film would interest you , showing great events similar to those in which you have just been taking part . " The Oliviers remained favourites of Churchill , attending dinners and occasions at his request for the rest of his life ; and , of Leigh , he was quoted as saying , " By Jove , she 's a clinker .
