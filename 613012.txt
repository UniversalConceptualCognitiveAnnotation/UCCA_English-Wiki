On his " Taxman " solo , McCartney commented , " I was very inspired by Jimi Hendrix . It was really my first voyage into feedback . " In 1990 , when asked who his favourite guitar players were he included Eddie Van Halen , Eric Clapton and David Gilmour , stating , " but I still like Hendrix the best . " He has primarily used a Gibson Les Paul for electric work , particularly during live performances .
